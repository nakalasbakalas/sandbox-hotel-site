# PHASE 3 HERO REPORT

## Design rationale

The homepage hero and navigation were redesigned to make Sandbox Hotel feel calmer, more premium, and more trustworthy within the first few seconds. The updated above-the-fold experience now leads with a clear boutique-stay promise, reinforces direct-booking confidence, and reduces ambiguity between browsing rooms and taking action.

## Hierarchy decisions

1. **Hotel promise first:** The H1 now communicates a calm, spacious stay in Nakhon Si Thammarat immediately.
2. **Action second:** The primary CTA is now a direct-booking action (`Check Direct Rate`) and the secondary CTA is a direct-contact action (`Call Hotel`).
3. **Trust proof third:** Review proof, direct-booking framing, and practical stay highlights now sit inside the hero panel before the user scrolls.
4. **Navigation clarity:** The header keeps the primary booking action visible, makes language switching feel intentional on desktop, and places call/LINE actions clearly inside the menu without crowding mobile.

## Trust elements used

- Genuine Google Maps rating signal
- Direct-booking / best-rate framing
- Practical stay proof:
  - spacious 28–32 m² rooms
  - fast Wi‑Fi
  - on-site parking
  - quiet comfort
  - convenient city access

## CTA strategy

- **Primary CTA:** `Check Direct Rate` → moves guests into the direct-booking flow
- **Secondary CTA:** `Call Hotel` → gives a fast-confidence path for high-intent guests
- **Supporting CTA:** `View Rooms` → serves lower-commitment browsers without competing with booking intent
- **Navigation CTA:** `Book Direct` keeps the direct-booking path obvious in the header and sticky states

## Mobile considerations

- Mobile hero height and image positioning were adjusted to preserve a strong building crop while keeping text readable.
- Hero trust proof stacks cleanly on smaller screens so the layout remains calm instead of cramped.
- Header actions stay compact on mobile by prioritizing the main booking CTA and menu, while keeping call/LINE options inside the menu.
- Touch targets remain large and thumb-friendly.

## Before / after conversion explanation

### Before

- The hero communicated the hotel name and a general comfort message, but the primary action was less clearly tied to direct booking.
- Trust proof existed, but the hierarchy did not emphasize why the stay is worth booking directly.
- Language controls were tucked away in the menu, making multilingual support feel less intentional.

### After

- The hero now states what Sandbox Hotel is and why it is appealing in one compact headline/subheadline pair.
- The primary CTA now clearly supports direct booking, while the secondary CTA supports immediate contact without confusing the main action.
- Trust is reinforced above the fold with rating proof, best-rate framing, and practical stay highlights.
- The navigation feels more polished and multilingual-friendly, with a visible desktop language switcher and cleaner mobile prioritization.
