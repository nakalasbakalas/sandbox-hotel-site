FAQ

How do I book the fastest?

Call or message us (LINE/WhatsApp) with check-in, check-out, guests, and preferred room type. We’ll confirm availability and total price.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in from 14:00 and check-out by 11:00.

What do I need at check-in?

A government-issued ID or passport is required for registration.

Can rates change by date?

Yes. Rates vary by date and season. Send your dates for an exact quote.

Do you have parking?

Yes, guest parking is available (subject to availability).

Is the hotel suitable for quiet rest?

We aim for quiet rooms; quiet-side allocation is available upon request when possible.

Is smoking allowed?

For guest comfort, please ask reception about the current smoking policy and designated areas.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation rules can vary by date/season. Confirm your dates with the hotel for the latest policy.
