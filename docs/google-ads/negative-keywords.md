## Review Rules

- Add exact negatives when one bad query appears and is clearly unrelated.
- Add phrase negatives only after multiple related bad queries appear.
- Do not block destination terms too aggressively; some early research queries can become hotel inquiries.
- Keep a change log in Google Ads notes for all broad phrase negatives.
