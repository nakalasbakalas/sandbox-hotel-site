## Copy Guardrails

- Do not claim a fixed price, discount, or guaranteed lowest rate unless the owner confirms it for the campaign period.
- Do not claim free cancellation, airport transfer, breakfast, pool, star rating, or a specific review count unless confirmed.
- Keep "best rate" copy tied to direct booking unless the account owner validates the exact wording.
