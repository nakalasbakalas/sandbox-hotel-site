Staying in Nakhon Si Thammarat

Southern Thailand's historic Gulf Coast city—a quieter destination with deep cultural roots.

Where We Are

Nakhon Si Thammarat is a historic city in Southern Thailand on the Gulf coast, 780 km south of Bangkok. One of the oldest cities in the country, it sits distinct from crowded Andaman beach resorts.

Why Visit

Nakhon Si Thammarat offers authentic Thai culture, impressive Buddhist heritage, and a relaxed local atmosphere. Home to Wat Phra Mahathat Woramahawihan—one of Thailand's most significant temples.

Nearby Attractions

  • Wat Phra Mahathat — 1,000-year-old temple complex
  • Bovorn Bazaar & Night Market — local food and evening browsing
  • Old City Walking Street — heritage shops and artisan stalls
  • Khanom & Sichon Beaches — quiet Gulf beaches 60–80 km away

Getting Here

  • By Air — NST Airport, ~1.5 hours from Bangkok
  • By Train — Overnight sleeper, ~12–14 hours
  • By Bus — Southern Bus Terminal, ~10–12 hours

Sandbox Hotel is a short drive from city centre. Airport transfers available—ask when you book.
