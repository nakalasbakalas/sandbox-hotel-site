Where is Nakhon Si Thammarat?

Nakhon Si Thammarat is a city in Southern Thailand, located on the Gulf of Thailand coast approximately 780 km south of Bangkok. It is the capital of Nakhon Si Thammarat Province and one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the country, with a recorded history spanning more than 1,000 years. The city sits on the Gulf Coast side of the Thai-Malay Peninsula, distinct from the Andaman Sea resorts of Phuket and Krabi.

Why Visit Nakhon Si Thammarat?

Unlike the crowded beach resorts of Southern Thailand, Nakhon Si Thammarat offers authentic Thai culture, impressive Buddhist heritage, and a relaxed local atmosphere. Travellers come for Wat Phra Mahathat Woramahawihan — one of the most significant temples in all of Thailand — as well as the unique local art forms of Nang Talung shadow puppetry and traditional nielloware silver crafts. The city is also a gateway for exploring the lesser-known national parks and coastlines of Nakhon Si Thammarat Province.

Key Attractions Near Sandbox Hotel

  • Wat Phra Mahathat Woramahawihan — one of the most important Buddhist temple complexes in Southern Thailand, dating back over 1,000 years.
  • Nakhon Si Thammarat National Museum — displays prehistoric artefacts, Buddha images, local crafts, and the history of the southern peninsula.
  • Bovorn Bazaar & Night Market — the best spot for local street food, Southern Thai dishes, and evening browsing.
  • Old City Walking Street — heritage shophouses and local artisan stalls every weekend.
  • Nang Talung Shadow Puppet Theatre — a uniquely Southern Thai performing art and UNESCO-recognised tradition.
  • Khanom & Sichon Beaches — quiet Gulf Coast beaches about 60–80 km from the city, ideal for a day trip.

How to Get to Nakhon Si Thammarat

  • By Air — Nakhon Si Thammarat Airport (NST/VTSN) has multiple daily flights from Bangkok (Don Mueang & Suvarnabhumi). Flight time approximately 1.5 hours.
  • By Train — overnight sleeper services from Bangkok's Hua Lamphong and Bang Sue Grand stations. Journey time approximately 12–14 hours.
  • By Bus — long-distance coaches depart from Bangkok's Southern Bus Terminal (Sai Tai Mai). Journey time approximately 10–12 hours.

Sandbox Hotel is a short drive or taxi ride from Nakhon Si Thammarat city centre. Airport transfers can be arranged — ask us when you book.

Nakhon Si Thammarat in Southern Thailand

Southern Thailand stretches from Chumphon in the north to the Malaysian border in the south. The region splits into two coastlines: the Gulf of Thailand coast (including Koh Samui, Koh Phangan, and Nakhon Si Thammarat) and the Andaman Sea coast (Phuket, Krabi, Koh Lanta). Nakhon Si Thammarat is one of the region's most historic inland and coastal cities — larger and more culturally rich than most visitors expect. It serves as an excellent base for exploring lesser-known Gulf Coast beaches, national parks, and authentic Southern Thai food culture.
